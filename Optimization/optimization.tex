\chapter{Introduction}

Optimization Problems can be written as the following form:

minimize $f_0(x)$

subject to $f_i(x) \leq b_{i}$, $i=1,2,3,...,m$

Here we call the vector $x = (x_{1}, x_{2}, ...x_{n})$ as \textit{optimization variable} of the problem.
